ClassSync seamlessly integrates your class schedule with the redesigned Viewbank College Intranet. When enabled, your next class will be displayed at the top of the page. Click it to open SneakPeek SneakPeek is a view consisting of the school map and your class list side by side. The class list shows you all current and upcoming classes for that day. Click a class in the list to highlight its room(s) on the map, or click a room on the map to see your classes there. There is also a button at the top to highlight all of your classes for that day on the map. for a full overview of the day.

For detailed instructions, see the ClassSync guide.
